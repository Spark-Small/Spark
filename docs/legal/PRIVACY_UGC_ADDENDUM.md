| Data type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Post title, body, media URLs | Community discussion, activity recaps | Until user deletes or moderation action |

| Reports (`reason`, optional detail) | Safety review, regulatory audit | ≥ 180 days |
